About the project

Have you ever heard about data mural? A data mural is a non-digital infographic form of art that aims to build data literacy and drive social change. When we think about data we always think about numbers, charts or graphs. But do you know that text is also a data and it is possible to make a data story out of text. We made a research about several interesting topics and out of those themes we selected one topic and made a data story out of it. Aim of painting the mural on the wall is to share the story with citizens and engage them with the data.
